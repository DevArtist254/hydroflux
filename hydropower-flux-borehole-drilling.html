Borehole refurbishment

We construct boreholes that offer services for a very long period of time without requiring any service or major maintenance. The refurbishment service is meant to ensure that the borehole remains in good condition and may also be a way of restoring the previous status of the borehole.

For example, there are those holes which when they are dug, will start reducing the initial amount of water that they used to produce, this means that they will need to be refurbished so as to restore their lost glory.

Borehole surveying

Surveying is an essential aspect of the whole procedure of drilling for our company which deals with borehole drilling in Kenya. This is because it helps to determine some of the impacts that the drilling will have on the nearby boreholes as well as those other features that are dependent on the same water. This surveying will also help to determine the long-term service of the water. The quality of the water will also be given by this analysis.

Our technicians carry out these services and are well-trained. Their services are world-class, with easy accessibility allowing anyone to get it. They also make use of equipment that is of new technology which makes their results accurate and reliable.

Aquifer testing

We carried out tests using the set standards of practice. This ensures that all the rules which need to be followed are observed and all the parameters which are used to do the testing are all approved by the board which deals with environmental matters.

The testing is done to trace large deposits of water and find out the extent to which the water table has extended. This gives a rough idea of the amount of water that can be drilled.
